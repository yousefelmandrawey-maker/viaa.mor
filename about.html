Our Story

Why Viaa exists.

We looked at how people share the most precious moments of their lives — through group chats, fading Instagram stories, screenshots buried in camera rolls — and thought: this doesn't match how those moments actually feel.

The moments that matter deserve permanence. They deserve beauty. They deserve a private space that belongs only to the people in them.

Viaa was built to give relationships a home. Not a post. Not a thread. A real, growing, cinematic space that tells your story the way it deserves to be told.

What We Stand For

Four pillars.
One purpose.

Private

Your story is not for the algorithm. Not for the feed. Not for strangers. Viaa pages are sealed by default — visible only to those you choose, protected by secret words, hidden from every search engine.

💎

Premium

Design is not decoration. It is how something feels. We build every theme, every animation, and every detail to feel expensive, emotional, and unforgettable. Because your story deserves nothing less.

Personal

No two Viaa pages are the same. Your names, your photos, your message, your music, your milestones. Every page is handcrafted around a real relationship. A real story. A real moment in time.

Memories fade. Social media posts disappear. Phones break. Viaa pages grow. We built Viaa Forever so you can keep adding memories, sealing capsules, and watching your story expand — indefinitely.

Privacy First

Your story
stays yours.

We designed Viaa around a simple principle: what happens between two people should stay between two people. Not on a public server. Not in a social feed. Not for anyone to stumble upon.

  • Pages are never indexed by search engines
  • Optional secret word adds a second layer of protection
  • Photos and videos stored securely, never shared
  • Time-locked memories that no one can open early
  • Access controlled entirely by you
  • Your data is not analyzed, sold, or advertised against
